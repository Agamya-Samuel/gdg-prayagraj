About GDG Prayagraj

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are community-led groups for developers interested in Google's technologies and platforms. GDG Prayagraj brings together passionate individuals in the region who want to learn, share, and grow in a collaborative environment.

Our mission is to build a strong tech community in Prayagraj and surrounding areas by organizing various events, workshops, hackathons, and meetups that foster learning and networking opportunities.
